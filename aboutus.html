About Us


Established in 2020, CityNest is currently Sri Lanka's most visited property and apartment listing website with over 1000 active Sri Lanka adsmin the market. Featuring houses, apartments, annexes, and lands for both rent and sale in all four corners of the island, CityNest.com is open to anyone to submit their property for sale, property for rent, land sales and any other property ads in Sri Lanka.

CityNest was started with the intention of providing an online platform for property owners, landlords and agents in Sri Lanka to advertise their property for free of charge. Our aim is to become the online property portal of choice for all Sri Lankans, where sellers and landlords will find buyers or tenants for their properties, and for buyers/tenants to find their ideal property easily and quickly.

We continuously strive to better our service and have invested heavily to provide the latest technology and features available to our advertisers and visitors to make their property buying and selling process simple and effective.

All ads on the site are posted directly by the owners or their agents and visitors contact these advertisers directly with their inquiries. We merely provide an online platform for buyers/sellers/tenants/landlords to find/sell property in Sri Lanka and are not estate agents or have affiliations with any.

We are also the hosts of The CityNest Show, Sri Lanka's largest real estate gathering, organized physically and virtually every year. It is the one stop place for a buyer's property needs and a key event looked forwarded to by property enthusiasts.

CityNest.com, house.lk and idealhome.lk are owned and managed by CityNest (Pvt) Ltd, incorporated in Sri Lanka under No. PV254180
